About EcoGrid

EcoGrid AI is an intelligent energy system that converts wastewater into electricity using Microbial Fuel Cells (MFCs) and optimizes power flow through AI forecasting. Our mission is to make every drop of waste count — creating a circular, sustainable energy ecosystem.

We collaborate with government agencies like TNEB to ensure that renewable power is efficiently stored and distributed, supporting smart cities and green communities.
